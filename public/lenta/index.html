Welcome to NFT Barracks

Train real NFT units paid with RONKE token. Each unit becomes an ERC721 NFT in your wallet with on-chain XP, battles, and kills history.

Pricing curve

Cost scales with global supply (Bitcoin-style). 10 RONKE base, grows to 1000+ as more units mint. Burn (death in battle) reduces supply, lowers cost for everyone.

Daily cap

Base 10 units per 12-hour period. +5 per held Ronkeverse NFT. Hard ceiling 100. Resets at 00:00 UTC and 12:00 UTC.

Batch multiplier

Up to 10 in 1 TX = no penalty. 11+ doubles cost per unit. Stay at 10 per batch for optimal cost.

Permadeath

NFT units MORTAL — if they die in battle, the NFT is permanently burned. Even high-XP veterans can die. Real stakes.
